  The Producers Guild of America (PGA) announced this year's winning motion picture, television, and digital productions at the 29th Annual Producers Guild Awards presented by Cadillac ceremony, which took place at the Beverly Hilton Hotel in Los Angeles. Closing the evening, the feature film “The Shape of Water” and its producers <pname>Guillermo del Toro</pname> and <pname>J. Miles Dale</pname> won the PGA's Darryl F. Zanuck Award for Outstanding Producer of Theatrical Motion Pictures.  This category is one of the most eagerly anticipated of season and widely considered a strong prognosticator for the Oscars®, as the Zanuck Award has matched the win for Best Picture 19 times in its 28-year history. Also, the films “Coco” won for feature animation and “Jane” won the theatrical documentary category.   The Hulu television series “The Handmaid's Tale” (Season 1) and its producers <pname>Bruce Miller</pname>, <pname>Warren Littlefield</pname>, and <pname>Leila Gerstein</pname> won the PGA's Norman Felton Award for Outstanding Producer of Episodic Television, Drama. Amazon's “The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel” (Season 1) and its producers <pname>Daniel Palladino</pname> and <pname>Dhana Rivera Gilbert</pname> won the Danny Thomas Award for Outstanding Producer of Episodic Television, Comedy.
