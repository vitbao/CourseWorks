FEB. 26, 2018
 The chef and restaurateur David Chang and the director Morgan Neville will discuss their new Netflix documentary series, “Ugly Delicious,” which made its debut on Feb. 23. It records Mr. Chang on a global food tour, illustrating the cultural power of food as he interacts with chefs, artists and political activists: “Ugly Delicious: A Conversation With David Chang and Morgan Neville,” moderated by Serena Dai of Eater.com, March 6 at 8 p.m., tickets $39, $35 and $20, 92nd Street Y, 1395 Lexington Avenue (92nd Street), 212-415-5500, 92Y.org.
